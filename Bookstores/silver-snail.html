Silver Snail

Silver snail is an independent comic bookstore with a very big selection of comic books. They also have figurines related to many different franchises. They are Toronto’s oldest comic bookstore; they used to be located near the Five Guys near TMU (formally Dundas Station).

As I entered the store, I was greeted by all the figurines from all different franchises: Marvel, Star Wars, DC, etc. Continuing past them they had an area for all kinds of manga and a big room in the back with a bunch of different comics. They also had a bin near the front with discounted comics for $2.
