Thunderstruck Bookstore

Thunderstruck is a small bookstore that’s sells comics, manga, graphic novels and artbooks.

This was one of the smallest stores I have ever been to. It had boxes full of books and comics filling the walls it was hard to walk through. There was a one-person wide path going through the store. The have a very wide variety of comics and manga.
