Type Books

Type Books is an independent bookstore known for their wide range in art and design books. They have three different locations in the GTA:

883 Queen St W
427 Spadina Rd
2887 Dundas St W
All three stores are co-owned by Joanne Saul and Samara Walbohm. Each store they have curated spaces to reflect on everything they love about books. They offer many literary programs and events for everyone to attend.

One of my favourite things about Type is their monthly book program called “Type in Translation”. This program dedicates a new book every month by an indie author from different countries. I have bought the first three books from this year (2026) and they were amazing, January was a German author, February was a Turkish author and March was a Croation author, each writing style was so different from the last, and they all had their own unique stories to them.
