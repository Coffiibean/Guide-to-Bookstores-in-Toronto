BMV

BMV is a well-known second-hand bookstore in Toronto. The first location opened in March of 1997, they were the “true second-hand bookstore” other than the iconic World’s Biggest Bookstore. They wanted to play fair prices for quality items which they still do till this day, by having books and CDs discounted because they were the publishers/creator’s overstock. With the attraction they got they were able to open their second store not even two years later at Yonge and Eglington. But their “superstore” location didn’t open till November 2005. They have 4 locations:

471 Bloor St W
10 Edward St
244 Queen St W
2289 Yonge St

Walking into this store was a lot all at once. I have heard that this store was big, but I didn’t realize how big. This store is about 4 floors and has such a huge selection of books. I went with a friend who loves history books and they had almost an entire floor just for that. They also have a little comic book section on the top floor with a great selection I walked out with about 6 comics.
