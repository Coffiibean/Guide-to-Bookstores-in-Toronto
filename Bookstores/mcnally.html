Ben McNally

Ben McNally is a family-owned independent bookstore. It first opened its doors, 19 years ago in 2007, at 366 Bay St. It was a beautiful and spacious, and got mentioned multiple times in the "best bookstore" list. Their stay at this location because only six months after they opened the financial crisis of 2008 hit. Then in 2019 the pandemic also affected their business which had them in a few temporary locations, 317 Adelaide Street East and at 400 King Street East. Finally in 2022 they opened the location they are currently in today 108 Queen St E.

My experience when entering this store was wonderful. They have such a cozy a curated space that makes you feel that you can walk around their tiny store forever. Though it is a tiny store they have such a wide variety of books. I am so excited to have a store near me that is curated with love and knowledge.
