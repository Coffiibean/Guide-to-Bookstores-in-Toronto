Swipe Design Books

Swipe Design Books is a store in the heart of the design district of Toronto. It is an independent bookstore that has the best and largest collection of design, advertising, and architectural books. But they don’t just carry books they also carry design related objects for people of all ages. These objects are both aesthetically appealing and functional. They carry books that can be bought and reference books.

I love going to Design to look at all the books they have to get references for work or a project that I am working on. They have a very modern vibe to the store, and it looks really cool.
