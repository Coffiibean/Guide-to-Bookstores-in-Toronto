Swipe Design Books

Little Ghosts is an independent bookstore known for their wide range of horror and monster thriller books. This is their one and only location. The store is run by a small staff of just 5 people. The store owner used to own a comic bookstore before Little Ghosts, but he was disappointed by the selection of horror and thriller books from other stores he decided to open up a store just for that.

My favourite thing about this store is that they publish their own books to sell in house, but also their wide selection of books, from a little scary to terrifying. They also hold their own events for many different things, workshops for writers, screenwriting workshops, and markets for other small businesses. They also have what they call Little Ghost ‘Fest an annual event where they take everything they do and put it together in one big room.
