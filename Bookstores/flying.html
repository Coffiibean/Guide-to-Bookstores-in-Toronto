Flying Books at Neverland

Flying Books at Neverland is a bookstore that is also publishes books and is a writing school. They don’t have as wide of a range of book than other stores, but they do have a well curated selection of books. Flying Books takes inspiration for their name from the fact that books transport you into another world, takes risks, and enlivens your world. They first opened their doors in 2015 on the first day of the CNE. They have 2 locations:
371 Queen St W
784 College St

This is the Queen St W location. I loved the vibe; there is a coffee shop to your left as soon as you enter and a shelf of books to your right. I was very busy which is a good thing, but it was also a little cramped as this location is quite small. I love how lively it was and the selection of books they had in the back of the store.
