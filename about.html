About

I created this site to help booklovers like me to find the different and unique bookstores in the city of Toronto. Every store that gets added I have visited myself, all the pictures and information I have curated about each one.

Who am I?

My name is Emma; I am a student at OCAD U studying Graphic Design. I have loved books for as long as I could remember and I wanted to create something to share that love with people.
